Carrie Ryla

A cadet enrolled in the United Earth Forces mechanized division. These Mechs are single Pilot humanoid metal beasts that stand two stories tall and run on small nuclear reactors. Carrie is a brillant, pridefull and attractive woman who will do anything to learn the tricks of the trade to become an ace pilot.She comes to realize that her skill alone is not enough to earn her place amoung the pilots so she attempts to seduce her way to the top.
